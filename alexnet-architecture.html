Key Innovations

🚀 ReLU Activation

First CNN to use ReLU (Rectified Linear Unit) instead of tanh/sigmoid. Speedup training by 6x.

📉 Dropout Regularization

Introduced dropout to prevent overfitting. Randomly sets 50% of neurons to zero during training.

⚡ Overlapping Pooling

Used stride 2 with 3×3 windows (overlapping) instead of traditional non-overlapping pooling.

🖥️ GPU Training

Pioneered training on GPUs - trained on 2 NVIDIA GTX 580 GPUs for 5-6 days.

🔄 Local Response Normalization

Inspired by biological neurons. Helps generalization but later found not essential.

🖼️ Data Augmentation

Used random cropping and horizontal flipping to artificially expand training data.
